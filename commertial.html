INDUSTRIAL ENERGY
REIMAGINED

We deliver high-efficiency solar PV systems for enterprise properties—scaling from factory rooftops to massive 500MW+ solar parks.

01 / Rooftop Efficiency

Smart Rooftop Infrastructure

Perfectly optimized for factories, warehouses, and commercial complexes. Solarica utilizes every square meter of available roof space to maximize your energy output and minimize grid dependency. Our rooftop solutions focus on fast ROI for industries and commercial establishments.

02 / Utility Scale

Ground-Mounted Solar Parks

Specialized large-scale installations often integrated into 500MW+ solar parks. These projects utilize shared government infrastructure to provide reliable, utility-scale power for major commercial operations. Solarica delivers these turnkey EPC services with maximum energy output in mind.

03 / Water-Based Innovation

Floating Solar Systems

Solarica implements advanced projects on reservoirs and water bodies to save valuable land space while improving panel efficiency through natural water cooling. These water-based solar installations are ideal for dams and canals, offering a sustainable future for land-constrained regions.

04 / Energy Resilience

Hybrid & Storage Systems

Solar combined with advanced battery storage or wind power ensures an uninterrupted power supply for mission-critical operations. Solarica specializes in AI-driven energy optimization tools that manage these hybrid systems to reduce operational costs and increase energy independence.

Investment Models

Choose the financial path that best fits your business cash flow and long-term energy goals.

Most Popular for ROI

CAPEX

Self-Owned System

Your business pays for the entire solar plant upfront. You own the asset from day one, allowing you to claim 100% of the tax benefits and maximize your long-term savings.

  • Zero monthly power bills
  • Full asset ownership
  • Highest total savings
Zero Investment

PPA / OPEX

Pay-As-You-Go

A third-party investor pays for, installs, and maintains the system. You simply buy the solar electricity it produces at a rate lower than the government grid.

  • No upfront cost
  • Zero maintenance hassle
  • Immediate savings
Flexible Credit

Financing

Lease or Loan

Install your solar system using customized bank loans or leasing options. This allows you to pay for the system in easy monthly installments while the energy savings help cover the costs.

  • Preserve your cash flow
  • Low interest options
  • Own it eventually
How We Work

Your Journey to Solar Power

Our comprehensive EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) process ensures a smooth transition from your first audit to long-term energy independence.

01

Survey & Smart Audit

We visit your site to analyze your roof or land and study your current electricity usage to find the perfect solar fit.

02

Custom Engineering

Our experts design a system using precision layouts and high-efficiency components to ensure you get the maximum energy output.

03

Approvals & Subsidy

We handle all the paperwork with government bodies and DISCOMs to secure your permits and available subsidies like PM-KUSUM.

04

Professional Installation

Our certified teams set up your solar panels and inverters quickly using world-class safety and quality standards.

05

Grid Synchronization

We test the entire system and connect it to the grid, officially turning your facility into a clean energy power plant.

06

AI-Driven Monitoring

We use real-time AI tools to track your energy generation 24/7, ensuring your system always performs at its best.
