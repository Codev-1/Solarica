PM-KUSUM Yojana

Empowering farmers with solar energy — irrigation, income generation, and energy independence for rural India.

PM-KUSUM (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan)

PM-KUSUM is a flagship Government of India scheme aimed at providing energy security to farmers, reducing diesel dependency, and enabling income generation through solar power.

MNRE
Nodal Ministry

State Agencies
Implementation
Objective: Replace diesel pumps, promote solar irrigation, and allow farmers to sell surplus power.

Components of PM-KUSUM

ComponentDescriptionTarget
AGrid-connected solar plants10,000 MW
BStandalone solar pumps20 lakh
CSolarisation of pumps15 lakh

Farmer Application Process

  1. Visit state renewable portal.
  2. Select PM-KUSUM component.
  3. Fill farmer & land details.
  4. Upload documents.
  5. Submit for approval.
