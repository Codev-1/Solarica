Types of Solar Projects We Deliver

At Solarica, we design, engineer and execute solar power projects across every major segment of the industry. From mega solar parks to rooftop systems for homes, our expertise covers the complete spectrum of solar deployment in India.

  • Utility-Scale Projects

    Large solar farms and mega solar parks feeding power directly to the grid. Ideal for government tenders, IPPs and large developers.
    Solarica undertakes full EPC for MW-scale ground-mounted plants.

  • Commercial & Industrial (C&I)

    Rooftop and ground-mounted systems for factories, hospitals, hotels, warehouses and institutions.
    We deliver EPC, CAPEX and RESCO models for enterprises.

  • Residential Solar

    Rooftop solar for individual homes and housing societies, aligned with government schemes such as PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana.
    Solarica provides subsidy guidance, installation & net-metering support.

  • Floating Solar

    Solar plants installed on water bodies like dams, reservoirs and canals, saving land and improving efficiency.
    We offer design & execution for water-based solar projects.

  • Hybrid Solar Systems

    Integrated systems combining solar with batteries, diesel or wind power for uninterrupted energy.
    Ideal for remote areas, industries and mission-critical operations.

Solarica works across all these solar project categories—delivering reliable, efficient and future-ready energy solutions for Government bodies, industries, institutions and homeowners.
