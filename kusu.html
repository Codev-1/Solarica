KUSUM Yojana

Cultivating Energy. Powering Farmers. Transforming India.

01. What is it?

Solarizing the
Indian Agriculture.

PM-KUSUM (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan) is a flagship scheme aimed at ensuring energy security for farmers while increasing their income by selling surplus solar power back to the grid.

02. Eligibility

Who can Apply?

Farmers

Individual farmers with valid land records.

Cooperatives

FPO (Farmer Producer Organizations) & Water User Associations.

Panchayats

Village communities and group of farmers.
