Mukhyamantri Saur Krushi Pump Yojana

Empowering farmers with solar-powered irrigation – saving electricity, reducing cost, and ensuring sustainable agriculture.

About the Scheme

Mukhyamantri Saur Krushi Pump Yojana aims to provide solar-powered irrigation pumps to farmers, enabling reliable daytime water supply without electricity bills.

Important: Eligible farmers can receive solar pumps with heavy government subsidy.

Subsidy Structure

Pump TypeCapacitySubsidy
DC Solar Pump3 HPUp to 90%
DC Solar Pump5 HPUp to 85%
AC Solar Pump7.5 HPUp to 80%

Application Process

  1. Visit official portal
  2. Register with Aadhaar & land records
  3. Select pump capacity
  4. Upload documents
  5. Track status online
Eligibility
Farmers with valid land records, electricity connection, and irrigation requirement.
Benefits
Zero electricity bill, day-time irrigation, eco-friendly farming, long-term savings.
